🎨 Activity 1

Learn about the Kaaba and build your own Kaaba replica using simple materials!

🌟 About the Kaaba

  • The Kaaba was built by Prophet Ibrahim (AS) and Prophet Ismail (AS)
  • Muslims face the Kaaba when praying
  • Muslims worship Allah alone — Muslims do not worship the Kaaba
  • The Kaaba is in Makkah
  • The Black Stone is part of the Kaaba structure

🕋 Step-by-Step: Build a Kaaba Replica

✨ Materials Needed

  • Small cardboard box or shoebox
  • Black construction paper or black paint
  • Glue or tape
  • Scissors
  • Gold paper or gold marker
  • Gray or silver clay/paper for the Black Stone
  • Ruler
  • Pencil
Step 1 — Choose the Box

Find a box shaped like a rectangle or cube.

Examples:

  • Shoebox
  • Small package box
  • Tissue box

This will be the main Kaaba structure.

Step 2 — Cover the Kaaba in Black

The Kaaba is covered with a black cloth called the Kiswah.

What to do:

  1. Measure each side of the box
  2. Cut black paper to fit
  3. Glue or tape the black paper around the front, back, sides, and top

Try to make the paper smooth and neat.

Step 3 — Add the Gold Band

Near the top of the Kaaba is a gold-decorated band.

What to do:

  1. Cut a long strip of gold paper
  2. Wrap it around the upper part of the Kaaba
  3. Glue it carefully

Optional: Draw simple decorations using a gold marker.

Step 4 — Make the Kaaba Door

The Kaaba has a golden door.

What to do:

  1. Cut a small rectangle from gold paper
  2. Glue it onto one side of the Kaaba
  3. Place it a little above the ground level
Step 5 — Create the Black Stone (Hajar al-Aswad)

The Black Stone is placed at the corner near the left of the Kaaba's door.

What to do:

  1. Roll a tiny piece of gray clay, black clay, or crumpled silver paper
  2. Shape it into a small oval
  3. Glue it near the lower corner of the Kaaba beside the door area

You can also draw a silver outline around it.

Remember:
• The Black Stone is called Hajar al-Aswad
• Pilgrims point toward it during Tawaf
• Muslims worship Allah alone, not the stone
Step 6 — Place the Kaaba on a Base
  1. Glue the Kaaba onto cardboard or foam board
  2. Leave space around it for Tawaf
